Material guide, by where it goes

The same rule as our packaging line: pick by where it goes, and the right film comes with it.

Your label ends up…We recommendWhy
On candles, soap & cosmeticsWhite vinyl or clear BOPPHandles oils, wax residue and bathroom humidity; clear film gives premium jars and bottles the no-label look.
On cold or hot drink cupsWaterproof BOPPShrugs off condensation, ice buckets and 90°C fills without smudging or lifting.
On jars & bottlesClear BOPPThe “no-label” look; waterproof, and we can reverse-print for inside-glass application.
On boxes & mailersRecycled paper (PCW)High post-consumer-waste stock, matches kraft packaging, curbside-recyclable with the box.
On pouches & sachetsMatte white vinylFlexes with the film, resists oil and freezer temperatures.
On a limited dropHolographic filmShelf standout with an anti-counterfeit feel. Costs more per unit; worth it for small collectible runs.

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to what growing brands ask us most often.

What is the minimum order?

100 units per design, same as every Dylign product. Order five designs of 100 each if that matches your shelf; there is no per-design fee.

When am I charged, and when do you print?

You pay at checkout, like any Dylign order. Right after, our prepress team builds your free digital proof and emails it — usually within 48 hours. We don’t print until you approve. If the proof isn’t right and we can’t fix it, you get a full refund.

How long until stickers are on my shelf?

Your free proof lands within 48 hours of checkout. After approval: 7–10 business days of production, then 5–8 days of tracked air freight. Plan for 3–4 weeks door-to-door; we’d rather quote that than promise a week.

What artwork do you need?

Best case: .AI, .CDR or 300 DPI PDF, CMYK, fonts outlined, under 10 MB. Real case: whatever you have — or hit “Design Now” and brief us. Our prepress team redraws blurry files free for simple marks, and quotes any design fee on the proof, before we print.

Are the materials durable and sustainable?

The BOPP films are waterproof and oil-resistant, so they hold up on bottles, jars and freezer pouches. The recycled paper stock is high post-consumer waste, and liners go back into our Dylign Terraveil™ program. Need documentation for a specific use — outdoor, freezer or otherwise? Ask us before you commit and we’ll tell you exactly what we can provide.

Can you match my packaging colors?

If we printed your boxes or pouches, your color profile is already on file. Otherwise mail us a sample; on-screen color lies, ink doesn’t. We’ll confirm the match on your free proof before anything prints.
