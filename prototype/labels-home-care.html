Frequently asked questions

The four things home & personal-care brands ask us most before they order.

What is the minimum order?

100 units per design, the same as every Dylign product. Run several designs of 100 each in one order — a candle, a soap and a serum — with no per-design fee and no plate or setup fee.

What is the lead time?

Your free digital proof lands within 48 hours of checkout. After you approve: roughly 7–10 business days of production, then tracked freight. Plan for 3–4 weeks door-to-door — we quote that honestly rather than promise a week.

Which material should I pick for my product?

Candles want a heat- and oil-resistant BOPP; soap and bath products want a waterproof vinyl; skincare looks best in clear film or FSC-certified recycled paper; oils and freezer products want an oil-resistant BOPP. Not sure? Pick the closest and we’ll confirm on your free proof.

What artwork do you need?

Best case: .AI, .CDR or a 300 DPI PDF, CMYK, fonts outlined, under 10 MB. Real case: send whatever you have, or hit “Design Now” and brief us. You pay at checkout, then we build your free proof — and any design fee is quoted there, before we print.
