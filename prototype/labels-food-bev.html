Food & beverage label FAQ

Quick answers to what bottling, brewing and pantry brands ask us most.

What is the minimum order?

100 units per design, same as every Dylign product. Running several flavors? Order a few hundred of each — every SKU still starts at 100, with no per-design plate fee or setup fee.

How long is the lead time?

Your free proof lands within 48 hours of checkout. After you approve: roughly 7–10 business days of production, then tracked freight. Plan for 3–4 weeks door-to-door — we’d rather quote that honestly than promise a week.

Are these waterproof and fridge- or freezer-safe?

Yes. The BOPP and vinyl films are waterproof and oil-resistant, so they hold up on the outside of bottles, jars and pouches through condensation, the fridge, the ice bucket and the freezer. Add gloss or matte laminate — it’s included. Tell us the exact conditions and we’ll confirm the right film on your proof.

Do you provide food-contact or safety documents?

Honestly: no. We print the label that goes on the outside of your packaging — the bottle, jar, can or pouch exterior. We do not currently certify direct food-contact use or provide migration or safety reports. If your use involves direct contact with food, ask us about your specific case and we’ll tell you plainly what we can and can’t do.
